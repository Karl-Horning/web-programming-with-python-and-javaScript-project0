About this site

Hello, my name is Karl and I created this site as part of CS50's Web Programming with Python and JavaScript course through edX.

I lived, worked, and studied in London for seven years.

During my time in London, I frequently visited the different museums and attractions that were available.

People often ask me about the best places to visit in London, so I decided to create this list along with the supplementary information on the links page to help people to plan a trip.
